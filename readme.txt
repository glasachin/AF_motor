-------PIN Configuration of DC Motor---------
Red – motor Power  (Exchange can control the motor positive and negative rotation).
Black – encoded power supply(VCC) – Positive (positive 3.3-5v).
Yellow – signal feedback (motor turns 2 signals).
Green – signal feedback (motor turns 2 signals).
Blue – encoded power(GND).
White – Motor power (Exchange can control the motor positive and negative rotation).
